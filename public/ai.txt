# Intentionality - Mindful Browsing Tool

Intentionality is a digital well-being tool designed to break the cycle of mindless scrolling and impulsive internet use. It prompts users to pause and reflect on their intentions before visiting distracting websites.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Mindful Prompts**: Asks "Why are you visiting?" to interrupt the dopamine loop.
- **Privacy First**: All data is stored and processed locally on the user's device.
- **Deep Insights**: Provides local analytics on browsing habits.
- **Cross-Platform**: Available for Chrome and Android (Waitlist for iOS/Firefox).
