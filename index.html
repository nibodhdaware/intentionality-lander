Frequently Asked Questions

How does Intentionality differ from other website blockers?

Intentionality goes beyond blocking—it makes you reflect on your purpose, helping you build lasting habits, not just avoid sites.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. Your data is stored securely and never sold. You control your information at all times.

Can I use Intentionality on multiple devices?

Absolutely! Sync your block list and analytics across all your Chrome browsers.

What happens if I want to access a blocked site urgently?

You can always proceed after reflecting on your reason—Intentionality is about mindful choice, not restriction.

How often are new features added?

We're always improving! Premium users get early access to new features as soon as they're ready.
